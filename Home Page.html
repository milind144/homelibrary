Types of Books and Reasons to Read more books

Self-Help

Sometimes in life we find ourselves stuck, it could be regarding your career, relationships, personality etc. In moments like these you can pick up a book from Self-Help category in order to learn more about your situation and how you can take advantage or improve your condition. Click on Books some of the titles that I would suggest for someone who might need help but is not keen for asking it from others.

Relationships

As Humans relationships are our core need, in order to not only survive but thrive as well. Building good relationships can save you a lot of drama and time and could really help in personal growth in long run, whereas if you sour relationship with your girlfriend / spouse, parents, friends etc. you can experience what hell really feels like. Therefore it is really important to understand the dynamics of human relations. The best part about this category is that when you start reading the following books you can really understand yourself and why you behave the way you behave, this knowledge alone can separte you from the crowd who have no idea why they act in certain manner.
